LOST FOREST

Lost forests are dense, mysterious woodlands that are often shrouded in mist and filled with ancient trees. These forests are typically found in remote areas and are home to a variety of rare and endangered species. The Lost Forest is a special place within the zoo where visitors can experience the tranquility and beauty of a natural forest environment.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the Lost Forest at the back of the zoo, near the entrance to the forest area.

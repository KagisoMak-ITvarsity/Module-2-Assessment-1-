PANDA

Pandas are large, gentle bears of the bamboo forests of China. They are known for their distinctive black and white markings and their preference for bamboo as their primary food source. Pandas are native to central China and are found in the mountain ranges of Sichuan, Shaanxi, and Gansu provinces.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the pandas at pen P15, right next to the amphitheatre.

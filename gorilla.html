GORILLA

Gorillas are large primates native to Africa. They are the largest living primates and are known for their strength and intelligence. Gorillas live in groups called troops, which are typically led by a dominant male known as a silverback. They are primarily herbivorous, feeding on leaves, stems, bark, and fruit. Gorillas are found in the dense forests of central Africa.

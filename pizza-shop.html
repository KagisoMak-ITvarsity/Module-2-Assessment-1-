MOS PIZZA

Mos Pizza is a local pizzeria that serves authentic Italian pizza. It's a popular spot for visitors looking for a quick and delicious meal. The pizzeria offers a variety of pizzas, from classic margherita to more adventurous combinations.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find Mos Pizza in the main food court area of the zoo.

WARTHOG

Warthogs are large, robust pigs of the African savanna. They are known for their distinctive warty faces and tusks. Warthogs are found in a wide range of habitats across sub-Saharan Africa, from grasslands to woodlands and scrublands. They are primarily nocturnal and are excellent diggers, often creating burrows for shelter.

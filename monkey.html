MONKEY

Monkeys are small primates of the family Cercopithecidae. They are found in a wide range of habitats throughout Africa, Asia, and Central and South America. Monkeys are highly intelligent and social animals, often living in troops of 10 to 100 individuals. They are known for their playful behavior and dexterity in using tools.

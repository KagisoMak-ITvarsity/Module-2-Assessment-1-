ANIMALS MAP PLACES

LION

Lions are large cats of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India. The lion is the only living species in the subfamily Pantherinae and is the most social of all big cats. Male lions are known for their distinctive manes, which are a sign of their health and dominance. Lions are apex predators and are known for their strength and hunting prowess.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the lions at pen L12, right next to the amphitheatre.

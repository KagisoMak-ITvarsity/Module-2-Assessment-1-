GEMSBOK

Gemsbok are large antelopes native to southern Africa. They are known for their distinctive appearance, with males having a dark brown coat and white markings on their face and legs. Gemsboks are well-adapted to arid environments and can survive without water for long periods. They are typically found in open grasslands and scrublands.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the gemsboks at pen G22, right next to the amphitheatre.
